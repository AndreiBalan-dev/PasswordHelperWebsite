   What is a secure password?

- A strong password is a password formatted, preserved and protected accordingly.

- A properly formatted password must contain many characters, with a wide variety between these, and essential for important passwords: be difficult to memorized, that is to avoid as much as possible the words from the dictionary.

- To keep a password safe, it is necessary to avoid the installation and execution of dangerous programs on your device, but also avoiding entering the password on websites which you do not trust.
Caution: Always be careful with websites of the type PHISING.

- Protection of a password is done at common level, because sometimes security breaches

- 2-Factor Authentication is required for a much better protection. If this function is not exploited, for each connection attempt you will receive an sms/email and the attacker will need a code that only you have access to. As long as you haven't lost your phone or email account, no account can be lost for life, even if its password has been changed.

  Why is it important to have a secure password?

- Having a weak password, your account automatically becomes more vulnerable, depending on how weak it is password, the account may be compromised.

- A risk factor is the security loopholes. A security breach happens when someone manages to enter a database that includes information about you, such as your name, password, email, etc.

- Even if the 2-Factor authenticator is important, and seems impossible to defeat, the attackers managed to passed over it several times in the past, and to everyone's surprise, the steps towards exploiting it functions were not advanced at all.

- On most websites, your passwords are encrypted, then added to a database. Although they are encrypted, some passwords are not difficult to crack if they are weak in terms of formation the password. Hackers use the Brute-Force Hash Decryption method that automatically goes through a list of passwords or through each individual ASCII character, encrypts it in the respective set of cryptographic hash functions, and if your password from an encrypted database (hash) looks exactly the same as the password he obtained, then he found out your password and can connect to your account.

  What are the encryption methods?

- Popular encryption methods are SHA256 and MD5.

- For defense against the verification and concatenation of two hash results between your password and the text encrypted by the hacker, through the "Brute Force" method (Brute-Force Hash Decryption), developers use HMAC (Hash-based Message Authentication Code) that gives the user and the server used by the user a key specific and unique private, different from other keys. The popular communication application "WhatsApp" uses a combination of SHA256 and HMAC, this combination is called AES-256 and it cannot be decrypted.

  What is phishing?

- Phishingul, vine de la "fishing" in engleza, adica a pescui. Acesta reprezinta o metoda prin care un atacator incearca compromiterea parolei a unei victime.

- Atacatorul creeaza un website similar cu alte pagini web pe care ai un cont creat, si cu un domeniu similar acestuia. Websiteul de tip phising este mai apoi redirectionat prin diferite metode catre victime, iar cei ce nu au in vedere linkurile pe care dau click vor fi pacaliti sa isi introduca parola pe aceste website-uri. Odata ce parola a fost introdusa, atacatorul va avea acces la aceasta.
